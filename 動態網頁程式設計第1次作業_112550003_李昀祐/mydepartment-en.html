National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University

Department of Computer Science

Department Introduction

National Chiao Tung University has always been a prestigious research-oriented university, and its information technology department is a cradle for the cultivation of the highest quality computer technology in the country. Established in 1960, the department set up the first computer research program in Taiwan, introduced the first computer in Taiwan in 1961, successfully developed the first electronic computer in the country in 1971, and has developed the world's first stamp recognition system, the first 3D computer vision car, and the world's oldest Chinese keyboard.


As the earliest and largest computer science department in the country, we have the most faculty members, the most comprehensive research areas, and the largest number of alumni. The department also provides a direct Ph.D. program and offers various scholarships. Choosing Chiao Tung University's computer science department paves a very broad road for your future career.


To cultivate high-quality master's and doctoral students engaged in advanced information technology research and enhance our country's position in the global information industry, this department was established. The professors within the department form several research fields, fully leveraging integrative power, increasing interdisciplinary collaboration, and inviting international scholars to teach and recruit international students, thereby strengthening international cooperation and contributing to research and development in the field of information.
